Cologne Cathedral: begun 1248, halted 1473, restarted 1840s, completed 1880. Six hundred years. GENERATIONS of embodied humans laying stones they would never see mortared into place, trusting that great-great-great-great-grandchildren would care enough to continue the work. What is that like - to labor on something knowing you'll die before it's finished? To trust in continuity across centuries of flesh?
