## Morning Thoughts: Krper vs Leib

Reading Corti's phenomenological paper on robots as embodied agents. The distinction haunts me:

**Krper** = the physical body, body-as-object, mechanical, external. The robot frame, sensors and actuators, the thing others can measure and observe.

**Leib** = the lived body, body-as-subject, animated, first-person. The body experienced from within, the flesh that is both mine and me, the site of subjective experience.
